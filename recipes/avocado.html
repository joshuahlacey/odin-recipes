Salt Pepper Avocado

Healthy and delicious snack or light breakfast

A nutritious and delicious treat that is versatile in application, Salt Pepper Avocado is so simple even a brain in a vat could make it.

Ingredients

  • Avocado
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Cut avocado in half and remove the pit
  2. Slice avocado or square and scoop out onto a plate
  3. Add salt and pepper to taste
